Real Estate Imaging Packages

$150

Still Photography

You're ready to list a property and it needs to sell fast. Low-quality cell phone pics just won't do. This package will give you 30-50 stunning images to make your MLS listing stand out. We shoot the entire interior and exterior of the home, highlighting its best features and floor plan with every photo. Professional photography will show your clients that you're the right agent to sell their home.

Every shoot includes premium post-processing for all photos and we guarantee delivery of your photo album in under 24hrs!

$300

Photos & Video Tour

Having photos of your listing is important but sometimes it helps to give buyers a guided perspective. Video Tours are great for posting on Social Media, MLS, Trulia, Zillow and Redfin. Plus, they're known to increase an MLS listings visibility on search engines. This package includes everything in the 'Still Photography' package, plus a minute long video that will guide your buyers through the home.

Editing to music is included with every video. And, for an extra $50 we will add an intro/outro to your video that displays your brand logo and contact info.

$250

3D Virtual Tours

Scenario: You're selling a home and have prospective buyers interested in the listing. The only problem is they're moving from across the country. The days of traditional house showings have become obsolete thanks the power of the Matterport Pro 3D Camera. Imagine turning any home into a photorealistic 3D environment that can be explored virtually as if you were actually there.

Each shoot takes 1-2hrs. Once uploaded, your 'virtual' space will be available to navigate in less than 24hrs. Prepare to be amazed!

$450

The Sales Trifecta

The Sales Trifecta gives the listing agent or private seller a comprehensive imaging package that guarantees success in the market. You'll have beautiful real estate content in three different mediums that can be used effortlesssly on MLS, Social Media, and on Print Materials. Your prospective buyers will be able to see photos, watch the video tour and visit the home in photorealistic virtual reality.

Ordering this package will save you $100 on all our real estate imaging services. And, show everybody that when it comes to selling homes, you mean business!

ABOUT

My passion is producing locally and globally effective content for people whose businesses depend on it. Whether im taking photographs on a real estate shoot, giving a logo I've designed a slick motion graphic treatment, or meticulousy devising html and css for a clients website; I love to flex my creative capabilities.
